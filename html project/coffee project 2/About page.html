COFFEE JUNKIE

The coffee junkie headquater and Tea Room is located in the city of benin, right in GRA sapele road.

We serve breakfast, light lunches, daily specials, homemade cakes, afternoon tea and delicious coffees. Our menu is simple and our food is cooked fresh daily. Where possible we use locally sourced produce, with seasonal specials that are updated regularly.

Breakfast is served every day until 11.30 and lunch from then onwards. Fridays are particularly indulgent at The Bow with our owner John making one of her fab cheesecakes.

Inside, the atmosphere is cosy, our Soho room has a laid back feel with boutique surroundings, or for a more Intimate affair book a table in our snug or Garden room. We are fully licensed so you can enjoy a glass of wine, beer or cider with your meal or in the warmer months join us for an al fresco drink in our garden.

The coffee junkie is a great pit stop for a coffee and something sweet on your country walk or stay a little longer and enjoy a happy lunch with us. Everyone is welcome whether it be the kiddies who love our shakes or your four legged friends who can enjoy a treat from the bar.

We are small and family run and very thankful for each and every one of our lovely customers.
